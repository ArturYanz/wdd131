Site Name

The name will be "Orchestral Family". I chose this name because the main focus of the site will be to explain what an orchestra is and the usual instruments and families of instruments that are involved.

Domain: orchestralfamily.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to show people general information of a orchestra; What it is, and the usual instrument involved on them.

Scenarios

  • What is an orchestra?
  • What are instrument families?
  • What instruments are involved in a orchestra?
